Our Mission Statement

Our goal is to bring the anime community together. We all grew up being able to disucess anime the way we discuss sports in a barbershop setting. Now that the world has caught on to the wave we want to bring that barber shop talk to the public and share some of our most heated and controversial takes. Dont blink or youll miss the fun.

We hope to be a home for those who may not have the in person anime community that we have we wanna interact and take opinons and takes from the peope. If we had to give an example we hope to be the First Take of anime. We want to bring the discussions you always wanted to have to the for front.
